Gene Expression: DNA → mRNA → Protein

Secondary Structure Prediction (Chou-Fasman)

Predicts alpha-helix (H), beta-sheet (E), and coil (C) regions using simplified Chou-Fasman propensity values for each amino acid.

H Alpha Helix E Beta Sheet C Coil
↓ Protein properties feed into drug docking
3
Drug Candidate Screening

Enter a drug molecule (SMILES notation). Lipinski's Rule of Five is evaluated, then a simplified binding compatibility score is computed against the target protein.
